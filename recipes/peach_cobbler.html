Steps

  2. Combine milk, sugar, flour, baking powder and salt in another bowl; beat until smooth - the mixture will be thin.

  4. Melt 1/2 cup butter in a 9x13 inch pan. Pour batter over melted butter. Scoop peaches out of bowl and spoon over batter. Sprinkle top with additional cinnamon and nutmeg.
